# Network Contacts at Target Companies

Referrals account for 60–70% of offers. Map this before cold-applying.

## How to use
- Warm: met in person or regular online contact
- Cold: connected on LinkedIn but never spoken
- None: no connection

For warm contacts: reach out for a referral or intel call before applying.
For cold contacts: engage with their content 2–3 times, then DM.

## Referral Request Template

```
Hi [Name],

Hope you're well. I'm actively exploring my next role and [Company] is at the 
top of my list — specifically the [role/team].

Given your experience there, would you be open to a 15-min call to share what 
the engineering culture is like? And if things look like a fit, I'd love a 
referral — happy to make it easy for you with a blurb.

[Your name]
```

Keep it short. Ask for intel first, referral second — less pressure, more yes.
