## How to use this file

1. After any learning session (even 30 min), write one entry
2. The "Connects to" field is the most important — cross-domain connections are your moat
3. If an artifact is obvious, stub it in `github-projects/ideas.md` immediately
4. If a content angle is obvious, stub it in `content/ideas.md` immediately
5. Once artifact is shipped and content is published, update Status to PUBLISHED
6. Monthly: scan for LEARNED items that have no artifact → build them
