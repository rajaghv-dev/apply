## Technical Deep-Dives by Domain

### Chip Design / RTL
- [ ] Explain your RTL design process end-to-end
- [ ] How do you approach timing closure?
- [ ] Walk through a DFT strategy for a complex SoC
- [ ] How do you handle clock domain crossings?

### AI / LLM
- [ ] How would you design a RAG pipeline for a legal document corpus?
- [ ] Explain your approach to LLM evaluation
- [ ] What are the failure modes of agentic systems and how do you mitigate them?
- [ ] How do you pick between fine-tuning vs. RAG vs. prompt engineering?

### Systems
- [ ] Design a distributed job scheduler
- [ ] How does the Linux scheduler work?
- [ ] Explain memory management in a modern OS

### General system design
- [ ] Design a job search aggregator (meta question — this system)
- [ ] Design a compliance document analysis system
- [ ] Design a real-time fraud detection system

## Offer Evaluation Criteria

When an offer comes, compare on:
- Base salary vs. market (Levels.fyi, Glassdoor)
- Equity (vesting schedule, strike price, cliff)
- Bonus structure
- Benefits (health, pension/PF, relocation)
- Role scope (growth potential, team quality)
- Manager quality (research the person)
- Company trajectory (funding, revenue, headcount trend)
