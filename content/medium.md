## SEO approach

Medium ranks in Google. Choose titles that match search intent:
- "How to [do X]" → tutorial intent
- "[Tool] vs [Tool]" → comparison intent
- "What is [concept]" → informational intent
- "[Number] things about [topic]" → listicle intent

Always include: the primary keyword in title + subtitle + first paragraph.
